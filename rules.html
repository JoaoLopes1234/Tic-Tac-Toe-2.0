Game Rules

Main Board and Mini Games:

The main board consists of 9 mini games, arranged in a 3x3 grid. Each mini game is a standard 3x3 tic-tac-toe board, referred to as a "mini game board".

Turns and Moves:

On your turn, you play in a square of a mini game. The square you play in determines which mini game your opponent will play in next. For example, if you play in the center square of your mini game, your opponent must play in the central mini game on the main board.

Victory Conditions:

Each mini game follows tic-tac-toe rules. Winning a mini game secures it for that player. The goal is to win three mini games in a row on the main board (horizontally, vertically, or diagonally).

Forced and Free Moves:

If the mini game corresponding to the forced move is already complete (i.e., already has a winner or is a tie), the player can choose any mini game to play in.
